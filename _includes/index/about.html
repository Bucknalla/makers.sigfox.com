SIGFOX Specifics

The SIGFOX network is designed for small messages sent every now and then. It is not appropriate for high-bandwith usages (multimedia, permanent broadcast).

  • Up to 12 bytes per message. Seems small, but that's actually more than enough for most of the uses cases.
  • Up to 140 messages per day. This limitation is introduced, in part, to comply with regulations. The european regulation governing the 868MHz band enforces a transmission duty cycle of 1%.
